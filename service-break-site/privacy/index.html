Privacy Policy

Effective July 16, 2026

Service Break sends you notifications about live tennis matches. This policy explains what information the app handles and why. It's short because we collect very little.

What we collect

No account, no name, no email. Service Break has no sign-up. The app creates an anonymous identity (a random ID from Google Firebase) so your settings can follow your device. We never know who you are.

Device and notification data. To deliver alerts we store: a random device identifier the app generates, your device's push-notification token (issued by Apple), and your app settings — favorite matches, alert choices, quiet hours, snooze, and league filters — with timestamps.

Match data is public. Scores and match states shown in the app are public sports information; nothing about the matches comes from you.

Diagnostics. The app currently keeps a small technical log on your device only, to help us fix problems. It is not transmitted. If we ever enable in-app analytics, we will update this policy first.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your information. To anyone, ever.
  • We don't show ads or share data with advertisers.
  • We don't collect your name, email, contacts, location, or photos.
  • We don't track you across other apps or websites.

Who processes data for us

Three service providers handle data solely to run the app: **Google Firebase (anonymous sign-in), Apple and Expo** (push-notification delivery), and Render (our server and database hosting). Each receives only what its job requires.

Retention and deletion

Settings live as long as your device uses the app. Deleting the app abandons your anonymous identity; the associated data becomes unreachable and is removed in routine cleanup. To request earlier deletion, email us with your request — since we can't identify you, we'll tell you what to send from the app so we can find the right record.

Your choices

Notifications can be turned off any time in the app or in your device's Settings. Everything the app stores about you exists only to deliver the alerts you asked for.

Children

Service Break is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version appears at this address with a new effective date. Material changes will be noted in the app.

Contact

Questions or requests: contact@service-break.com
